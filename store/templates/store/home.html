Understanding Our Products

WHAT IS EXPORT
SURPLUS?

Export surplus garments are clothes that were originally manufactured for export to international brands or retailers, but were later sold in local markets instead of being shipped abroad.

These garments come directly from factories that produce for top brands in the United States, United Kingdom, and Europe. They carry the same quality, stitching standards, and fabric as what you'd find in branded stores abroad — at a fraction of the price.

Tirupur, known as the "Knit City of India," is the production hub for thousands of global garment brands. We source directly from these factories, ensuring every piece is authentic and top-quality.

📦

Extra Production

Factories produce extra pieces beyond the ordered quantity as buffer stock — these become surplus.

🔍

Minor Defects

Tiny stitching issues that don't affect wearability — rejected by brands but perfectly fine to wear.

❌

Order Cancellations

When international orders get cancelled, finished inventory is released into local markets.

🏷️

Label Mistakes

Packaging or labeling issues make pieces unsellable in foreign markets but perfect locally.

📋

Leftover Stock

After fulfilling export orders, remaining inventory is sold as surplus — same quality, lower cost.

💎

Same Quality

Made in the same factories, on the same machines, with the same materials as exported goods.

⚡ WE DEAL ONLY IN ORIGINALS

At AJAI PRANAV TEX, we take authenticity seriously. Every single garment we sell is 100% genuine export surplus — directly sourced from Tirupur's export factories. We do NOT deal in copies, imitations, first copies, or any fake products. What you buy from us is the same quality as what international brands sell abroad.
