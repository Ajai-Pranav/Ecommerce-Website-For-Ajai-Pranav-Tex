Who We Are

AJAI PRANAV TEX


We are AJAI PRANAV TEX, one of Tirupur's most trusted dealers in genuine export surplus garments. Located in the heart of India's garment capital, we are uniquely positioned to source the best quality export surplus clothing directly from factories.

Tirupur produces garments for some of the world's biggest brands in the United States, UK, and Europe. We work closely with these factories to bring you surplus stock that meets international quality standards — at prices that make sense for India.

Whether you're a retailer looking for wholesale stock or an individual wanting premium brand-quality clothing at affordable prices, AJAI PRANAV TEX is your one-stop destination.
